User Manual

1 How to Generate Signals

  • Step 1 (Calibration): The AI requires data to understand the current game volatility. Enter the result of the last 5 rounds (Time and Multiplier) into the "Add Round Data" section.
  • Step 2 (Analysis): Once 5 rounds are added, the "Get Next Signal" button becomes active. Click it to start the server connection simulation.
  • Step 3 (Result): Wait for the analysis phases (hashing, pattern recognition) to complete. The system will output a predicted high multiplier.

2 Betting Strategy Guide

Target Time

This is the exact time the AI predicts the high round will occur. Ensure you place your bets 10 seconds before this timestamp.

Safe Exit (Auto-Cashout)

The "Predicted Odd" is the potential maximum. DO NOT wait for it. Always set your Auto-Cashout to the "Safe Exit" value (e.g., if prediction is 12.00x, Safe Exit might be 9.50x).

Confidence Score

90%+: Aggressive Bet.
80-89%: Moderate Bet.
<80%: Skip or Low Bet.

3 The Feedback Loop

After every signal, you must report the result to keep accuracy high.

WIN Button Click this if the plane flew HIGHER than the "Safe Exit". It generates the next signal immediately.
LOSS Button Click if it crashed early. You will be asked to enter the actual crash odd. This re-calibrates the algorithm.
